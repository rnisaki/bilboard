From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
This year Usher took the top two spots with "Yeah!" and "Burn", plus the twelfth with "Confessions Part II". All three singles were from his Confessions album.

This is a list of Billboard magazine's Top Hot 100 songs of 2004.[1]
